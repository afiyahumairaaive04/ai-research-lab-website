Featured Research Projects

Examples of current and completed research efforts under the AI Research Lab.

Ongoing

Smart Crop Disease Detection

Using deep learning and image analysis to identify crop diseases from leaf images for early intervention.

  • Technology: Python, CNN, OpenCV
  • Outcome: Faster visual diagnosis support
Completed

Bangla Sentiment Analysis

Developing natural language processing models to classify opinions from Bangla text data accurately.

  • Technology: NLP, Transformers, Python
  • Outcome: Improved local language text analysis
Ongoing

Autonomous Indoor Robot

Designing a robotic system that can move intelligently in indoor environments using sensors and AI logic.

  • Technology: Robotics, Sensors, Embedded AI
  • Outcome: Smart navigation prototype
