Driving the Future of Intelligent Systems

AI Research Lab of the CIS Department explores Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Robotics, and Data Science to build practical, responsible, and innovative solutions for academia and industry.

Explore Research View Lab Overview

Mission

To create impactful AI research, support interdisciplinary collaboration, and prepare students and researchers to solve real-world problems with emerging technologies.

Core Research Domains

Our lab focuses on modern AI areas that connect theory, experimentation, and applied innovation.

ML

Machine Learning

Developing predictive and intelligent models from structured and unstructured data.

DL

Deep Learning

Designing neural network based solutions for complex classification, detection, and automation tasks.

CV

Computer Vision

Teaching machines to interpret images, videos, patterns, and visual signals accurately.

NLP

Natural Language Processing

Building systems that understand, analyze, and generate human language intelligently.

RB

Robotics

Combining AI and control systems to support smart robotic behavior and autonomous action.

DS

Data Science

Extracting insights from data to support research decisions, analytics, and innovation.
